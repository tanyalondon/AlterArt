Paint and Draw in Shoreditch

Studio Sessions

Painting Sessions

A studio painting session with emphasis on materials, techniques and safe studio practices. The works created during the course may utilize subjects from observation and imagination, applying design and composition principles, and drawing skills. Sessions to learn construction techniques and skills to build painting supports, and how to stretch, size and prepare a canvas surface also available.

Parent & Child

Sessions are geared towards the parent & child who love to make art together. Parent & child will be involved in activities that enhance fine motor skills, creativity, and imaginations!

Life Drawing

This is a life drawing course with the human figure as a visual prompt through which participants can unlock their own creativity. Focus on spatial concepts, technique and materials in a practical, hands-on class, combining traditional life drawing practices with experimental drawing ideas.

Still Life

This is a practical course, and you will spend most of class time drawing. The course focusses on drawing from direct observation and from photographs, using a variety of materials and techniques, similar to the live drawing session.
